The Investigation Workbench

The design set. A governed, human-in-the-loop workbench for any production investigation — its engine runs a versioned playbook through guided, audited phases, and a human approves every write. Incident triage is the first use case.

Infrastructure Resilience & Operations · v2 (current) · governed at the boundary · regulator-grade audit · earned, reversible autonomy · no vendor or domain lock-in
